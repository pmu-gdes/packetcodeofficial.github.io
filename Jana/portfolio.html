Redesign a BOOK

In this assignment, choose one of the typography styles (Egyptian hieroglyphs, Greek, or Roman) and use it to redesign the front, spine, and back cover of any chosen book. The objective is to authentically capture the typography aesthetics of that specific era and incorporate them into the book's design.

Create office STATIONERY

For this assignment, Develop a new brand and design office stationery/collateral. The stationery set should include a logo, business card, letterhead, envelope, notebook, and a gift item such as a pen or USB drive. Additionally, you may also incorporate another relevant object into the design.

Designing a landscape POSTER

In this assignment, Design a poster featuring a retro Nike Jordan Air theme. The poster should primarily consist of text, comprising 70% of the design, while images should make up the remaining 30%.

Visual POETRY

In this assignment, use typographic manipulation techniques to enhance the message conveyed by each word. Leveraging computer programs, you can creatively manipulate written words to amplify and emphasize the ideas they represent. Engage in the unique manipulation of ten words to showcase their meaning in a distinct and impaction manner.

Redesigning a brand IDENTITY

This assignment is about choosing a Saudi woman who owns a business, shop, etc., then redesigning the whole brand identity, starting with the business name if necessary, and creating a hybrid logo that combines Latin & Arabic calligraphy, till the brand mock-ups.

Create a children STORY

For this assignment, Create a bilingual children's storybook in both English and Arabic. The story should be engaging and captivating for young readers. The book should consist of four pages in English and four pages in Arabic, with the illustrations remaining the same while only the languages are edited. Additionally, the book must include cover pages in both English and Arabic.

Drawing a portrait that transmits INNOCENCE.

For this assignment, the task was about creating a portrait that emphasizes the face and its expression. The primary goal is to capture the likeness, personality, and mood of the individual, with a particular focus on conveying their innocence.

Gift warp design with Arabic patterns and GEOMETRY

In this assignment, the task is to design an A3-sized gift wrap with Arabic patterns and geometry, utilizing various design forms, elements, traits, and formal relationships. Incorporate repetition, rhythm, grouping, and colors to enhance the design.

Poster design with 10 textures and complementary COLORS

For this assignment, create a poster design of A3 size incorporating a minimum of 10 distinct textures. The theme of the poster is open-ended, allowing for creative freedom. You may freely choose colors and textures and enhance the design by adding complementary colors.

Create a book design for one of your favorite BOOKS

For this assignment, Design 2 double pages in InDesign: Front and back cover with enticing graphics and internal pages that visually interpret the book while respecting copyright laws.

Design the E-newsletter for a  UNIVERSITY

In this assignment, Create an e-newsletter for a university that serves as a regular email communication, delivering news, information, and updates to its members, employees, or individuals interested in the university's activities.

Create a 3D object and animate it with a GIF

In this assignment, the goal is to take a 2D image or unique design and transform it into a 3D representation. Subsequently, the 3D design will be animated by creating an animated GIF.

Create a unique LANDSCAPE

This assignment entails crafting a captivating landscape using the pen tool in Illustrator. Within this landscape, you will combine essential elements, including majestic mountains, a tranquil pond, a radiant sun, and clouds. The goal is to create a harmonious composition that showcases the beauty of nature. Focus on maintaining simplicity while using the pen tool to bring these elements to life with precision and creativity.
