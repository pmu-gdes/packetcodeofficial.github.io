About Me

I'm Naila Labban, a graphic designer with a deep commitment to the art of design. I was born and raised in Saudi Arabia "Dhahran" .

I honed my skills at Prince Mohammed bin Fahd University, where I studied and immersed myself in the world of visual arts.

I truly love what I do for a living and that keeps me driven to sharpen my skills and become a better designer everyday. My goal is to always bring same drive and energy into every project I work on, no matter how big or how small
